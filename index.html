Chi Chi's Trampoline Jumping
Tips and Tricks:

Ready to unleash your inner chimp and bounce to the moon?
Hold onto your bananas jumpaholics, because Chi Chi, the master of mid-air mayhem at SkyJumper, is
here to share some secret tips and tricks for the ultimate bouncing experience!

Tip
#1

Monkey See, Monkey Do

Don't try flips and tricks you haven't mastered yet. Start with little hops like a baby chimp, then learn to stop safely before you go all Tarzan.

Tip
#2

Centre Stage is the Sweet Spot

The middle of the mat is where the magic happens. Stay away from the edges and frame, just like Chi Chi does during his gravity-defying jumps.

Tip
#3

Land Like a Monkey, Not a Coconut!

Jumping off the trampoline is a no-no! Use the ladder like a responsible chimp and avoid any gravity-defying exits that could end in a coconut-style thud!

Bonus Tip: Want to impress everyone with your epic bouncing skills? Practice, practice, practice! The more you bounce, the
better you'll become, and soon you'll be soaring through the air like Chi Chi himself!

Remember, bouncing should be a blast, so follow these tips, bounce responsibly, and most importantly, have
fun! Now go out there and show the world your inner chimp!
